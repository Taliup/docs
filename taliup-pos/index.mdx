Taliup POS runs on PAX payment terminals and turns them into a full point-of-sale system. Use it to ring up sales, accept card payments, manage open orders, and keep track of transactions — all connected to your Taliup HQ account in real time.

<Frame caption="Taliup POS on a PAX terminal showing the Register screen.">
  ![Taliup POS Register screen on a PAX terminal](/images/taliup-pos/register.png)
</Frame>

## Prerequisites

Before setting up Taliup POS on any terminal, the following must be completed first:

1. **PaxStore setup** — terminals created, activated, and apps pushed. See [Taliup HQ → Devices → PAX → PaxStore Setup](/taliup-hq/devices/pax/paxstore).
2. **Taliup HQ merchant setup** — Entity created and each terminal registered as a Device with its TID. See [Taliup HQ → Devices → PAX → Add Devices](/taliup-hq/devices/pax/add-devices).

<Warning>
  Do not open Taliup POS on a terminal for the first time until both steps above are complete. Logging in before device registration results in no users appearing on the login screen.
</Warning>
