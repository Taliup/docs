Taliup POS supports two PAX terminal configurations. The setup steps and app architecture differ between them — select the guide for your device below.

| Device | Form factor | Apps |
|---|---|---|
| **PAX Elys** (L1400 + A3700) | Two-device pair — L1400 cashier terminal + A3700 customer display | Taliup Elys (L1400) · BroadPOS Manager, acquirer BroadPOS app, Taliup Companion (A3700) |
| **PAX A920 Pro** | Single standalone terminal — all-in-one | BroadPOS Manager · acquirer BroadPOS app · Taliup POS |

## Prerequisites — complete before first login

Both steps below must be finished before you open Taliup POS on the terminal for the first time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="PaxStore setup">
    Terminals must be created and activated in PaxStore, and the correct apps pushed to each device. The **TID** (terminal ID) assigned in PaxStore is required for the next step.

    [PAX PaxStore Setup →](/taliup-hq/devices/pax/paxstore)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Taliup HQ device registration">
    Each terminal must be registered as a **Device** in Taliup HQ using its TID from PaxStore. The merchant Entity must also exist, and at least one user (Employee) must be assigned.

    [Add PAX Devices in HQ →](/taliup-hq/devices/pax/add-devices)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Do not open Taliup POS on the terminal until both steps are complete. The login screen will show no users if the device TID is not registered in Taliup HQ.
</Warning>
