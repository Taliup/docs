In Taliup HQ, a merchant account is called an **Entity**. Creating an Entity is the first step in onboarding a new merchant. Once an Entity exists, you can register devices, configure payment settings, and grant staff access.

<Frame caption="The Create Entity form in Taliup HQ.">
  ![Create Entity form showing fields for business name, plan selection, and merchant details](/images/taliup-hq/create-entity.png)
</Frame>

## What happens when you create an Entity

- A merchant account is created in Taliup HQ.
- An **Employee** user is created automatically and assigned to the Entity. This user appears on the terminal login screen so staff can sign in on their first shift.
- The Entity is assigned a **Plan** that controls which features are available and determines the billing tier.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Taliup HQ">
    Go to [taliuphq.com](https://taliuphq.com) and log in with an account that has permission to create Entities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Entities">
    Click **Entities** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new Entity">
    Click **+ Create Entity**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the required fields">
    Fill in the merchant's details, including the business name and contact information. Select a **Plan** for the merchant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create Entity** to save. The Entity is now created and the Employee user is available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's next

After creating the Entity, register the merchant's payment terminals as **Devices** in Taliup HQ. Each device requires the **TID** (terminal ID) from your device provider.

- [Register PAX devices →](/taliup-hq/devices/pax/add-devices)
- [Configure Plans & Features →](/taliup-hq/onboarding/plans-features)
