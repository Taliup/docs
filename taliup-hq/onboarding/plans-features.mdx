A **Plan** controls which features are available to the merchant — such as reporting, online payments, and Virtual Terminal access — and determines the billing tier. The plan is selected when you create the Entity. Change it here if the merchant upgrades, downgrades, or moves between trial and live billing.

## How to get here

**Entities** → find the Entity → **Manage** → **Settings** → **Plans & Features** tab.

## Change the plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Plans & Features">
    From the **Settings** page, click the **Plans & Features** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a new plan">
    Under **Change Plan**, select the plan you want to apply to the merchant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply">
    Click **Apply** to save the change. The merchant's available features update immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Changing a plan may enable or disable features the merchant is actively using. Confirm with the merchant before downgrading.
</Note>
