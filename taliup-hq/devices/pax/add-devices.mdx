After creating terminals in PaxStore and noting each **TID**, register each terminal as a **Device** in Taliup HQ. Taliup POS uses the device TID to request the list of assigned users from Taliup HQ — device registration must be complete before staff log in on the terminal for the first time.

## How many device records do I need?

| Device | Device records to create |
|---|---|
| **PAX Elys** (L1400 + A3700) | Two — one for the L1400 (cashier terminal) and one for the A3700 (customer display) |
| **PAX A920 Pro** | One — for the A920 Pro |

<Warning>
  For PAX Elys, both the L1400 and A3700 TIDs must be registered in Taliup HQ **before** opening Taliup POS on the L1400 for the first time. Logging in before device registration is complete will cause the login screen to show no users.
</Warning>

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Entity">
    In Taliup HQ, click **Entities** in the sidebar, find the merchant's Entity, and click **Manage**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Devices tab">
    Click the **Devices** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new Device">
    Click **+ Create Device**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the device details">
    Fill in the required fields:

    - **Device Name** — a descriptive name for this terminal (for example, "Front Register" or "L1400 Cashier").
    - **Device Type** — select the correct device type for this terminal.
    - **TID** — enter the TID from PaxStore for this terminal.
    - Complete any remaining required fields.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create Device** (or **Save**) to register the device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Repeat for additional terminals">
    For PAX Elys, repeat steps 3–5 to create a second device record for the A3700 using its TID from PaxStore.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | What to try |
|---|---|
| No users on login screen / cannot log in on first setup | Confirm the terminal TID exists in PaxStore and a matching Device record exists in Taliup HQ before opening Taliup POS. Confirm the Entity has assigned users (an Employee user is created automatically when the Entity is created). |
| Demo Mode passcode rejected | Confirm the passcode with your ISO, confirm the terminal has internet access, and confirm the terminal TID is registered in Taliup HQ. |
| Terminal keeps logging out with 503 errors | The marketplace subscription may have expired. Renew the subscription in PaxStore or use Demo Mode. |
| Merchant locked out of Taliup POS | An ISO Administrator may have disabled the merchant's **Location** in Taliup HQ. Re-enable the location under **Settings → Locations**. |
