Taliup HQ ([taliuphq.com](https://taliuphq.com)) is the central dashboard for merchants and ISOs. Use it to manage entities, configure payment settings, register devices, review transactions, and control every aspect of your Taliup account.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Entities" icon="building-2">
    Create and manage merchant accounts (Entities), assign plans, and configure locations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Devices" icon="credit-card">
    Register PAX terminals and other payment devices with their PaxStore TIDs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payments" icon="dollar-sign">
    Configure tips, dual pricing, surcharging, and online payment gateways.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Virtual Terminal" icon="monitor">
    Process card-not-present payments directly from the HQ dashboard.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
