Taliup's `taliuphq-php` SDK gives you everything you need to accept payments in your PHP application. Redirect customers to a Taliup-hosted checkout page, verify incoming webhook signatures with HMAC-SHA256, and handle structured errors — all without building or hosting your own payment UI.
